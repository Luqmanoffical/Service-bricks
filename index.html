ServiceBricks is a fully open-source platform, designed to empower developers and businesses by providing all the tools needed to build microservices with ease. It is completely free to use, whether for personal projects, educational purposes, or full-scale commercial applications.

Licensed under the MIT license, ServiceBricks offers the flexibility and freedom for customization and integration within your own tech stack. Our commitment to open-source principles ensures transparency, accessibility, and a collaborative approach to continuous improvement.
